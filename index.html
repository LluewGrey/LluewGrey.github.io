My name is Lluew Grey

    Having come from a farm in upstate New York originally, I spent 20+ years in the US Navy and now reside in the beautiful US Pacific Northwest. I write fiction in multiple related genres including Fantasy, Historical Fiction, and Science Fiction.

I am also a nature photographer and digital artist.
Some of my artwork is available on my redbubble shop, along with various artwork I commisioned for my books.


I record, edit, and host two podcasts both are available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Amazon Music, and just about anywhere you listen to podcasts

  • The Random Table Podcast: A live Play RPG Podcast where we play various different game systems including Kids on Bikes, Dragon Bane, Vaeson, Alien, ElfQuest, and more!

  • Indie Author Journey Cast: Where my cohost and fellow Author TL Merrybard and I have discussions with Authors, Game Designers, Musicians, and Publishing Industry Professionals

I can also be found on YouTube Where I post videos about anything that intrests me... from math to making brownies



Any one who may be intrested in writing, or any other project that could use a good editor, I highly recommend Andrew Malinski at Beyond: Editing and Writing Services LLC
